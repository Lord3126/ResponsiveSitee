About The Vantage

Gorgeous and supremely athletic, the Vantage is the entry-level Aston Martin, if there’s such a thing. But there’s nothing basic about this stunning British sports car, with its looks to kill and a 503-hp twin-turbo 4.0-liter V-8. An eight-speed automatic is the sole transmission; a seven-speed manual is coming. An electronically controlled limited-slip differential and adaptive dampers are standard. The exterior theatrics continue inside with upscale materials and countless custom options.
